Simple Syrup Recipe

Source: www.allrecipes.com by Shaggy

Learn how to make simple syrup to use in homemade cocktails and other drink recipes. It's easy to make with just two ingredients!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup water

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine sugar and water in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Bring to a boil, stirring, until sugar has dissolved. Allow to cool.
  4. Pour into a clean jar and seal with a tight-fitting lid; store in the refrigerator for up to one month.

Bon Apetite!
